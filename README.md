Welcome to the Record Shop App (Beta) where you can filter and find all the records,
cd’s and music accessories all in one place. 

The website has all available items visible.

You can Log In but if you click on profile or add to cart it will fail 
but you can view your cart despite it being empty. 

There are 4 methods of filtering options.

You can select your Category, Minimum Price, Maximum Price, and Genre.

The Category Filter will give you 4 Options.

Show All - Gives You All Categories.

Vinyl Records - Will give you all the music we have on Vinyl.

CDs - Will provide the music we have in the cd format. 

Music Accessories - Will show all items that are supportive of music playing such as
record players, speakers, record sleeves, etc.


The price filters will give you two bars you can drag with the top bar allowing you to filter by the 
Minimum price and bottom bar filtering the Max price(Don't let the Minimum text fool you).

Finally we have the genre filter that applies to all categories but you won’t get results if the “Music Accessories” filter is on.
